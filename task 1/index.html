Fashion Style:

Fashion style refers to a distinctive and consistent manner of dressing and presenting oneself that reflects personal taste, cultural influences, and societal trends. It encompasses various elements, including clothing, accessories, footwear, hairstyles, and overall aesthetics.

  • Wide-leg trousers
  • Sustainable materials
  • Oversized blazers
